Haboro Contrast

Intro Set Font Body Design

For my Type Specimen project I went along and chose the typeface, Haboro Contrast. Haboro Contrast has the description of having the same “clean geometric shapes” of a similar typeface Haboro Sans but with a slight difference. This typeface was designed with the idea of having an elegance that its sister font is without. To me something with elegance is a thing that stands outs and leaves the viewer in awe. It stands out in a way that isn’t loud and, in your face, but done with a level of subtly. On the adobe site where the typeface was found, a lot of the examples have the typeface in a lot of neutral and darker colors. They also further emphasis the themes of the font with words such as “gentle”, and “confident” The creator of the font can be found here!
